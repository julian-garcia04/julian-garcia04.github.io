About Us

A UA Family, For UA Families

We are a Tucson family and have had 2 Wildcats graduate from the University of Arizona. We know what it's like to send your kid off to school and hope they end up somewhere decent.

We are dedicated to providing premium housing to students. Our homes are kept up, fully furnished, and close to campus. Everything is included — WiFi, utilities, washer and dryer. Each student signs their own lease, so your child is only responsible for their own rent.

We pick up the phone. If something needs fixing, we take care of it. Come take a look before you decide — we're happy to show you around.
